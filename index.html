CONTROLS AND F1 STEWARDS RULES

Touch Screen (Mobile)

  • Left Thumb: Steering Left / Right
  • Right Thumb: REVERSE (left), GAS (right of reverse), BOOST (multiplier)

Keyboard (Desktop)

  • Accelerate (GAS): W or Up Arrow
  • Reverse / Brake: S or Down Arrow
  • Steering: A / D or Left / Right Arrows
  • Boost: Shift or Spacebar

F1 Stewards & Penalty System

Passing all checkpoints in order is mandatory. Leaving track limits (all 4 wheels on grass) triggers official warnings. 3 track limit offenses add a +5.0s time penalty to your final race time! Shortcut corner cutting adds +10.0s.
